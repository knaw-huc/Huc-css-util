A dataset of Data Huygens ING

Digital Web Centre for the History of Science

Dataset compiled by the Digital Web Centre for the History of Science, an initiative of Huygens ING. The dataset consists of four subsets:

  • Past members of the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences (KNAW)
  • Scientific Instrument Makers in the Netherlands
  • The network of Carolus Clusius (1526-1609)
  • Members of Dutch scientific societies
