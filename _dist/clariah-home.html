Clariah Anansi

Bridge to networked research data

ANANSI is the central data connection between the three primary CLARIAH domains (Linguistics, Social & Economical History and Media Studies). ANANSI facilitates the connectivity of structured (meta-)data served by the research infrastructures constructed in these domains. Furthermore, ANANSI integrates data with large scale existing data infrastructures outside CLARIAH - both within and outside the humanities.

These datasets are powered by Timbuctoo.
